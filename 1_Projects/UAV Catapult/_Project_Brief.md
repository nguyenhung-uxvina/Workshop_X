## Muc Tieu

> Thiet ke he thong phong UAV kieu Catapult (launcher) de trien khai tren tau tuan tra,
> nha gian, dao nho, tram bien phong Viet Nam.

## Thong So Yeu Cau

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| MTOW UAV | 5-120 kg |
| Toc do phong | 15-25 m/s |
| Sea-state max | 4 (tau lon), 2-3 (tau ca/nha gian) |
| Gio max | 12-14 m/s |

## Cong Nghe Launcher

| Cong nghe | MTOW | Toc do | Phu hop |
|-----------|------|--------|---------|
| Bungee/Spring | <12 kg | 15-18 m/s | Ngan han, prototype |
| Pneumatic | 40-100 kg | 15-25 m/s | Trung han |
| Hydraulic | <120 kg | 15-30 m/s | Dai han |
| EMALS (dien tu) | Large | High | R&D dai han |

## Lo Trinh

1. **Ngan han (3-6 thang):** Prototype bungee/pneumatic, thu nghiem tau tuan tra + dao
2. **Trung han (6-18 thang):** Launcher dien/hydraulic, nha gian, chong an mon
3. **Dai han (18-36 thang):** EMALS R&D, modular interface, san xuat quy mo
